Singapore Tour Package

Perfect Time To Visit Singapore

Discover the jingle holiday bazaar in Singapore, where you can embark on an amazing sightseeing adventure! This vibrant city offers fun-filled activities for all ages. Admire iconic landmarks such as the grand Marina Bay Sands and the enchanting Gardens by the Bay. Don't forget to snap picture-perfect moments at Merlion Park and enjoy a leisurely stroll along the picturesque Singapore River. Savor the mouthwatering local delicacies at bustling hawker centers, and be sure to try the famous chili crab dish. Shopaholics will love Orchard Road, a shopper's paradise with countless boutiques and malls. Immerse yourself in Singapore's rich culture by exploring Chinatown, Little India, and Kampong Glam. Delve into history at Fort Canning Park and the National Museum of Singapore. For some relaxation and excitement, unwind at Sentosa Island's beautiful beaches and thrilling theme parks. Singapore beautifully blends modernity and tradition, promising an unforgettable holiday for everyone. So, pack your bags and get ready for an extraordinary adventure in this amazing city.

Latest Tour Packages

botanic gardens tour

If you are a nature enthusiast or simply looking to experience stunning sights, the Singapore Botanic Gardens is a must-visit destination. Spanning over 82 hectares, the garden boasts an impressive collection of flora and fauna, including orchids, ferns, and towering trees. Visitors can explore the many themed gardens, unwind by the lake, or attend one of the many events or concerts held throughout the year.

gardens tour

Gardens by the Bay is a must-see attraction in Singapore, spanning over 101 hectares of land filled with a stunning display of flora and fauna. Visitors can enjoy the iconic Supertrees, conservatories such as the Flower Dome and Cloud Forest, as well as the outdoor gardens. The site offers a variety of dining and shopping options while taking in the breathtaking views.

little India tour

Little India is a vibrant and lively neighborhood in Singapore that offers a unique experience with its rich culture, colorful buildings, and delicious food. Tourists should not miss visiting the Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple, Campbell Lane's colorful shophouses, and the buzzing 24-hour Mustafa Centre. One can try authentic Indian cuisine and also shop for souvenirs and traditional clothing.

marina bay sands tour

Marina Bay Sands is an iconic destination in Singapore that offers a wide range of entertainment, shopping, dining, and accommodation options. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of the city from the SkyPark Observation Deck, take a leisurely stroll along the promenade, or catch a show at the state-of-the-art theater. It is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves sightseeing.

merlion park tour

Merlion Park Tour Merlion Park is a well-known destination for tourists in Singapore situated in the Marina Bay region. The park features an iconic statue of the Merlion, a creature that is half-lion and half-fish and is a symbol of the city-state's heritage and culture. Visitors can take photos and enjoy the stunning views of the bay and the city skyline from the park.

national gallery tour

National Gallery Tour The National Gallery Singapore is an excellent destination for art enthusiasts and anyone looking to explore interesting things. Situated in the heart of Singapore's Civic District, it boasts the largest public collection of modern art in Southeast Asia, featuring more than 8,000 artworks from Singapore, Southeast Asia, and beyond. In addition to admiring the art pieces, visitors can participate in various exhibitions, tours, and events.

The resort boasts a vast 22,500 square meter indoor ski area that offers real snow throughout the year.
